| :---- | :--: | -----: | ------: | :----------------------------- |
| M0    |  —   | 512 MB |    5 GB | Free, shared                   |
| M10   |  2   |   2 GB |   10 GB | Dedicated, dev/staging         |

| M50   |  4   |  16 GB |  160 GB | High throughput                |
| M80   |  8   |  32 GB |  750 GB | Large production               |

Cell alignment is controlled by colons in the divider row — left, center,
right.
